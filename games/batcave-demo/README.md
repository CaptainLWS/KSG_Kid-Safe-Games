# Harley & The Chaos Sisters: Batcave Demo

## 🎮 About This Game

Explore the neon-lit Batcave with iconic DC characters! Choose your hero and collect glowing stars while enjoying a relaxing, pressure-free adventure.

**Perfect for:** Kids of all ages, fans of Batman universe, casual gamers looking for chill gameplay.

---

## 🕹️ How to Play

### Getting Started

1. **Pick Your Character** – Choose from 5 playable heroes:
   - 🎀 **Harley Quinn** – The chaotic, playful one
   - 🐱 **Catwoman** – Sleek and stealthy
   - 🌿 **Poison Ivy** – The nature lover
   - 🦇 **Batman** – The classic detective
   - 🎩 **Alfred Pennyworth** – The calm support

2. **Move Around** – Use:
   - **Arrow Keys** (↑ ↓ ← →), OR
   - **WASD keys** (W, A, S, D)

3. **Collect Stars** – Walk onto glowing stars to collect them
   - Your score increases with each star
   - More stars respawn automatically
   - Keep playing as long as you want!

### Game Objectives

- ✅ Explore the Batcave
- ✅ Collect as many stars as you can
- ✅ Have fun (no winning or losing!)
- ✅ Try different characters

### What Makes It Special

- **No Time Pressure** – Play at your own pace
- **No Fail States** – You can't lose or get stuck
- **No Damage** – Completely safe gameplay
- **Infinite Stars** – Keep collecting forever
- **Character Variety** – Each hero plays the same but looks different
